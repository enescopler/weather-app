About

Weather App is a web application that allows users to easily access real-time weather information. It retrieves weather data for cities around the world from reliable sources, providing quick and accurate information to users.

Features:

  • Current weather: View up-to-date weather information for your location or selected cities
  • Weather forecast: Get weather predictions for up to 4 days.
  • Detailed data: Temperature, wind speed, humidity.

User-Friendly Design:

With its simple and intuitive interface, the app is easily accessible for users of all ages.
